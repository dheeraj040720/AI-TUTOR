Why it Matters

Mental health influences how you think, feel, and manage the transition to adulthood.

Research shows that positive mental health is linked to higher GPA, better concentration, and increased motivation. It helps in building resilience for future career challenges.

Common Challenges

Burnout, anxiety, and depression can hinder focus and motivation.

Signs include social withdrawal, sleep disturbances, and extreme exhaustion. Early identification is key to preventing academic decline and absenteeism.

Support Strategies

Connecting with friends, engaging in physical activity, and university workshops.
